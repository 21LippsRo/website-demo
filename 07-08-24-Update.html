Lincoln-Reagan Day Dinner and Independence Day Celebration!
July 8, 2024
July 8, 2024 – Over the past month, I have been involved in several events across Warren County including the Lincoln-Reagan Day Dinner hosted by the Warren County Republican Party and the Franklin Independence Day Parade with Michelle Teska for State Representative. Although months away from the general election in November, the local Republican party is as excited as ever to elect Republican candidates up and down the ballot.

In June, I attended the Warren County Republican Party’s Lincoln-Reagan Day Dinner with guest speaker, Riley Gaines. Attending the dinner and private reception, I met many conservatives from across the state and Warren County who came out to meet Riley. During her keynote speech, Riley gave an extraordinary speech and shared her heartbreaking story of her victory being stolen and an attempt to keep her silent.

This Independence Day I joined Michelle Teska for State Representative (55th District) in the Franklin Independence Day Parade. Franklin is my hometown and I am very proud to support an amazing woman for State Representative to replace term-limited (now Vice Chair of the Republican Party) Scott Lipps. Walking through the parade allowed me to see so many familiar faces from my city, and see so many new faces. Hopefully, when construction is done in downtown Franklin, future Independence Day Parades will be bigger and better than ever before.
